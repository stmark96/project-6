Developer Trivia

When was HTML first introduced?

Tim Berners-Lee, a scientist and academic, was the primary author of HTML, with the assistance of his colleagues at CERN, an international scientific organization based in Geneva. The first version of HTML was formally published in June 1993.

(Source: Bellis, Mary. "The History of HTML and How It Revolutionized the Internet.")
